Forge an Item

The local blacksmith is known across the world as one of the best -- Too bad he's completely insane. When we order swords he gives us shields, when we ask for shields he gives us coloured marbles, and when we tried to ask for coloured marbles (just for fun), he gave us a delightful mug with a kitten on it.

Over the years the Adventurer's Guild has at least partially figured out how to reason with this unreasonable character. You'll need 3 things:

  1. Rare Ore to forge with
  2. Alcohol to 'lubricate the mind' (as he puts it)
  3. Words of encouragement
Through some combination of these three things, maybe, maybe, you'll get what you want (but you'll probably have to experiment).

Good Luck
